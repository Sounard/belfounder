unzip hackathon-agent-pack.zip
cd hackathon-agent-pack

# 2 · start Claude Code here
claude

Why it doesn't hallucinate your event into a ditch

The pack ships a CLAUDE.md doctrine file with the load-bearing numbers baked in — 200% overbooking, the judge formula, prize parity, the five killer deadlines. Override anything: it complies, and writes a ⚠ Deviation note with the documented risk into the generated file.

Prerequisites

Claude Code installed (npm install -g @anthropic-ai/claude-code). No repo, no build, no config — the skills auto-load from the folder.

The skills

Six specialists in your terminal

🎬

/scaffold

The flagship. Ten-question interview → the complete event/ directory, dated to your calendar, with the three next physical actions.

💶

/sponsors

Prospectus, target list matched to your theme, personalized outreach emails, a pipeline CRM that lists every follow-up due — and the 48-hour post-event report.

🗓

/run-of-show

Minute-by-minute schedule with an owner per block: threshold, 15-min opening, protected build blocks, checkpoint, demos, double close.

⚖️

/judging

Computes your judge count, writes the rubric and calibration script, generates scoring sheets — or designs a no-judging format when your goal calls for it.

📣

/comms

The full attendee sequence — acceptance to recap, ten messages, drafted and dated, with the ONE consolidated paperwork deadline enforced.

🔁

/debrief

Surveys, the sponsor metrics report, winner-story drafts, retro template, and activation of the follow-up container — the week that decides if your event compounds.
