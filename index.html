“Hackathons sometimes produce technologies, and they always produce subjects.” — Lilly Irani, Science, Technology & Human Values
~9% of “hackathon code” is written at the hackathon
~7% of projects survive six months — by default

Judged as software factories, hackathons are a scam. Judged as identity factories, they're the best deal in civilization: deadline + strangers + witnessed demo = a person who has publicly enacted “I am someone who builds.” Design for the conversion; make shipping the designed add-on — done right, survival jumps to 25–40%.
